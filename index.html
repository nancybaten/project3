My Home Page

One of my hobbies is watching true crime.

I consider it a hobby of mine because it takes up most of my time.

I've always been fascinated with what happens in court and the retracing of events before the crime was commited.

My hobby started off as pure curiosity but transformed to genuine interest.

True crime made me read more and want to educate myself on certain topics that get brought up in cases to understand them better.

One of the podcasts I listen to frequently because of its thorough research is named Rotten Mango

Some of the reasons I regularly watch true crime is to:

  • Stay informed on what is going on around the world
  • Find ways to advocate for people
  • Hear the story of survivors
